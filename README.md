# RandomizingGroupsSimulator
- This simulator randomizes groups of people based on how many groups the user wants. 
- Press the "Submit Groups' Number" button to randomize groups
- After typing the name, you can press "enter" also. 
# Updates
- You can separate 2 people from being in a group together, but you can also separate a different group of 2 people too, from being in a group together.
---Example: Separating Jack and Jill, Separating Jack and Jake, separating Bill and Molly is allowed
- Hilight over a certain name in the names group to delete the name.
- Hilight over a certain group and delete it for that certain display.
